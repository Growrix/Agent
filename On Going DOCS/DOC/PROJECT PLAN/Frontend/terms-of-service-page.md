# Terms of Service Page

## Page Definition
- Purpose: define commercial and usage expectations for services and digital products.
- Target audience: service clients and product buyers.
- Primary CTA: Contact for Clarification.
- Secondary CTA: Read Privacy Policy.

## Sections In Visual Order

### 1. Terms Header
- Content: effective date, agreement summary, and quick section index.
- Components: utility hero, anchor nav.

### 2. Services Terms
- Content: proposals, scope boundaries, revisions, communication, delivery expectations.
- Components: content blocks.

### 3. Product Purchase Terms
- Content: licenses, usage limits, support scope, updates, refunds where applicable.
- Components: structured content cards, alert block.

### 4. Payments and Billing
- Content: deposits, milestone payments, Stripe processing, taxes, failed payment handling.
- Components: content section.

### 5. Liability and Acceptable Use
- Content: limitations, client responsibilities, prohibited misuse of products or systems.
- Components: content blocks.

### 6. Contact and Related Policies
- Content: contact route, privacy link, support route.
- Components: CTA row, utility links.

## State Requirements
- Anchor nav highlights the current section and supports keyboard navigation.

## Responsive Adaptation
- Mobile uses a simplified legal reading layout with a sticky jump menu.

## SEO and Metadata
- Title: Terms of Service.
- Description: Review the commercial, licensing, payment, and delivery terms for services and digital products.

## Conversion Path
- Terms review -> clarity -> proceed with booking or purchase.
