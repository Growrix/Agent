# Blog / Resources Page

## Page Definition
- Purpose: capture SEO traffic and educate homeowners on common plumbing problems.
- Target audience: homeowners, landlords, and property managers researching a problem.
- Primary CTA: Read an Article.
- Secondary CTA: Request Quote.

## Sections In Visual Order

### 1. Blog Hero
- Content: practical plumbing advice, emergency guidance, and maintenance tips.
- Components: hero, search bar, topic chips.

### 2. Featured Guides
- Content: blocked drains, hot water faults, leak detection, burst pipes, water pressure, seasonal maintenance.
- Components: article cards, category badges.

### 3. Service-Linked Articles
- Content: every article should route into a relevant service or area page.
- Components: related link panels, CTA strips.

### 4. Local SEO Topics
- Content: suburb or city focused maintenance and service advice for Australian homes.
- Components: location cards, reading list.

## State Requirements
- Search supports loading, no-results, and error states.
- Article cards should stay readable on mobile and not rely on hover.

## SEO and Metadata
- Title: Plumbing Tips, Repairs, and Home Maintenance Guides.
- Description: Helpful plumbing articles for Australian homeowners, landlords, and property managers.
