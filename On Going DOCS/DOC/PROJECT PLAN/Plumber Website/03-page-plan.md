# Page Plan

## 1. Home Page
- Purpose: convert urgent and planned visitors fast while showing trust and service breadth.
- Primary CTA: Call Now or Request Quote.
- Secondary CTA: WhatsApp or Ask AI.
- Sections in order:
  - Hero with response-time promise, license/insured badges, and a quick symptom picker.
  - Floating bottom-right action cluster explanation visible on first load.
  - Service category grid: emergency plumbing, blocked drains, hot water, leak detection, burst pipes, toilets and taps, commercial, maintenance.
  - Postcode checker and service-area teaser.
  - Reviews summary with star average and recent local testimonials.
  - Before/after proof or job gallery.
  - Process strip: call, assess, quote, fix, confirm.
  - Maintenance membership or planned-service offer.
  - FAQ teaser and final conversion band.

## 2. Services Overview
- Purpose: help visitors self-select the right service quickly.
- Primary CTA: View Service Details.
- Secondary CTA: Request Quote.
- Sections:
  - Urgent services versus planned services.
  - Residential, commercial, and strata groupings.
  - Common symptoms cards that map to services.
  - Trust and response-time strip.
  - Related areas and contact CTA.

## 3. Service Detail Template
- Purpose: explain one plumbing problem clearly and move the user to action.
- Primary CTA: Call for Help or Get a Quote.
- Sections:
  - Problem overview and common symptoms.
  - What causes it.
  - What the plumber does on site.
  - When it is urgent.
  - Pricing guidance or price-band explanation.
  - Photos, proof, and review snippets.
  - Booking and contact CTA.
  - Related services and areas.
- Example services:
  - Emergency plumbing
  - Blocked drains
  - Burst pipes and leak detection
  - Hot water systems
  - Toilet and tap repairs
  - Bathroom, kitchen, and laundry plumbing
  - Commercial and strata plumbing
  - Maintenance plans

## 4. Areas Served
- Purpose: prove local coverage and capture postcode searches.
- Primary CTA: Check My Postcode.
- Secondary CTA: Contact Us.
- Sections:
  - Postcode checker.
  - State / city / suburb map or list.
  - Coverage states: available now, planned service, not covered.
  - Fast response messaging by zone.
  - SEO-friendly area pages for suburbs and major cities.

## 5. Reviews
- Purpose: build trust with local proof.
- Primary CTA: Call or Book.
- Secondary CTA: Read More Reviews.
- Sections:
  - Rating summary and source badges.
  - Review cards with suburb and service type.
  - Before/after or job-result proof.
  - Licensed / insured / same-day trust strip.
  - Final contact band.

## 6. Quote Request
- Purpose: reduce friction for non-urgent jobs and capture enough detail to quote well.
- Primary CTA: Submit Quote Request.
- Secondary CTA: WhatsApp for Faster Help.
- Fields:
  - Name
  - Phone
  - Email
  - Postcode
  - Service type
  - Urgency
  - Short description
  - Photo upload
  - Preferred contact method
- Sections:
  - Multi-step quote estimator.
  - Price-range guidance where appropriate.
  - Privacy note and response expectation.
  - Confirmation state with next-step guidance.

## 7. Book Service
- Purpose: schedule planned work or inspection visits.
- Primary CTA: Confirm Booking.
- Secondary CTA: Call for Immediate Help.
- Sections:
  - Calendar or slot picker.
  - Service selection.
  - Preferred date and time.
  - Address and access notes.
  - Booking confirmation and reminders.

## 8. Contact
- Purpose: let users choose the best communication method.
- Primary CTA: Call Now.
- Secondary CTA: WhatsApp.
- Sections:
  - Phone
  - WhatsApp
  - Email
  - Business hours
  - Service area
  - Emergency availability note
  - Contact form for non-urgent enquiries

## 9. AI Concierge
- Purpose: answer common questions and route users to the right next step.
- Primary CTA: Ask the AI.
- Secondary CTA: Escalate to WhatsApp.
- Sections:
  - Prompt chips for blocked drain, no hot water, leak, burst pipe, quote, coverage, and booking.
  - Suggested next actions.
  - Privacy and handoff messaging.
  - Fallback to call or human chat.

## 10. FAQ
- Purpose: remove objections about pricing, response time, coverage, and guarantees.
- Primary CTA: Call or Book.
- Secondary CTA: Ask AI.
- Topics:
  - Emergency response
  - After-hours calls
  - Areas served
  - Quote process
  - Photo-based estimates
  - Licensing and insurance
  - Payment and invoices
  - Maintenance plans

## 11. Blog / Resources
- Purpose: capture SEO traffic and educate homeowners.
- Primary CTA: Read an Article.
- Secondary CTA: Request Quote.
- Topic ideas:
  - How to tell if a blocked drain is urgent
  - Signs your hot water system is failing
  - What causes low water pressure
  - How to prevent pipe leaks in older homes
  - Seasonal plumbing checks for Australian homes

## 12. About
- Purpose: humanise the business and show why the team is trustworthy.
- Primary CTA: Call the Team.
- Secondary CTA: View Reviews.
- Sections:
  - Local story
  - Licences and qualifications
  - Service promise
  - Team / truck / workshop photos
  - Response-time and workmanship values

## 13. Privacy Policy and Terms
- Purpose: provide trust and compliance clarity.
- Primary CTA: Contact Us.
- Secondary CTA: Return Home.
- Sections:
  - Data handling for quote, booking, analytics, and AI chat
  - Payment / invoice terms if applicable
  - Service limitations and warranty language
  - Consent and contact preferences

## 14. 404 Page
- Purpose: recover lost visitors and return them to a useful path.
- Primary CTA: Go Home.
- Secondary CTA: Call or Chat.
- Sections:
  - Reassuring message
  - Quick links to services, areas, quote, booking, and reviews
  - Persistent contact options
