## Asset Brief
- Photo direction: real installations, rooftops, inverters, technician workflows, customer walk-throughs.
- Subject guidance: include people interacting with installed systems and real property context.
- Required ratios by surface:
  - Hero media: wide cinematic and portrait companion pair.
  - Card thumbnails: uniform landscape ratio.
  - Testimonial support images: square crop.
  - Case study galleries: mixed ratio allowed with masonry-safe framing.
- Allowed fallback sources: licensed free-stock photography with authentic field captures.
- Banned fallback sources: generic AI mockups, staged call-center stock, non-solar industrial renders.
- Alt text intent: action, context, and equipment relevance in concise language.
