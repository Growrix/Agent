## Technical Implementation Notes

### Global Components (Shared Across All Routes)
- **Header/Topbar:** Logo, nav links, WhatsApp/call CTA, theme switcher, mobile menu  
- **MobileBottomNav:** Icon-based tab navigation (5 tabs: Home, Services, Portfolio, Contact, More)  
- **Footer:** Standardized footer with contact options, social links, attribution  
- **Toast Notifications:** For lead form submissions, contact tracking  
- **Modal Infrastructure:** For lead capture, video testimonials, image gallery  

### Route-Specific Components
- **Home:** Hero, trust badges, services preview, testimonials carousel, CTA band  
- **Services:** Service cards, benefits grid, comparison table, ROI explanation  
- **Portfolio:** Image gallery, before/after slider, case study cards, video embeds  
- **Contact:** Multi-step form, live chat widget, map, hours/availability, calendly embed  
- **FAQ:** Accordion, search, categorization, video answers  
- **Financing:** Calculator embed, comparison chart, loan partner logos  
- **Blog:** Post cards, search, filtering, related posts, author bios  

### Lead Capture Infrastructure (All Routes)
- **Top-of-page CTA banner** (dismissible): "Get Free Solar Assessment" or "Schedule Consultation"  
- **Sticky header CTA:** Phone + WhatsApp buttons, always visible on mobile  
- **Sidebar CTA widget** (desktop, >= 1024px): "Chat with Expert" or "Book Consultation"  
- **Page-exit intent modal** (optional): Special offer or assessment reminder  
- **Form auto-save:** Draft preservation for multi-step contact form
