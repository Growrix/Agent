# Preset: High-Energy Launch

Bold, vibrant, high-contrast aesthetic. Pages feel exciting, urgent, and confident.

## Character

- bold
- vibrant
- high-energy

## When To Use

- Product launch pages
- Campaign landing pages
- Startup marketing sites
- Event or conference pages
- Limited-time offer pages
- Any context where excitement and urgency drive the design

## Visual Rules

### Typography

- Posture: editorial display-heavy
- Headlines: bold or black weight, large scale, dominant presence
- Body: standard readable, supporting the headline drama
- Display text: oversized statements, uppercase allowed for short headlines
- Number callouts: large, bold metric displays for impact data

### Color

- Accent: vibrant, saturated tones — electric blue, hot pink, vivid orange, bright green
- Backgrounds: bold — dark bases with vibrant accent overlays, or bright hero backgrounds
- Contrast: maximum — strong foreground/background, purposeful intensity
- Gradients: bold, directional gradients as structural elements (not decoration)
- Allow multicolor accents if controlled and intentional

### Shape

- Language: balanced to sharp
- Radius: ranges from sharp (0-4px) to medium (8-12px) — not soft
- Cards: may have accent borders, glow effects, or highlighted states
- Buttons: high-contrast, prominent, slightly oversized primary CTA
- Dividers: bold or colored

### Surface

- Depth: layered with intensity
- Shadows: colored shadows allowed (accent-tinted)
- Glow effects: allowed sparingly for CTA and feature emphasis
- Surface treatment: may include noise, grain, or bold texture on hero sections
- Dark + neon combinations allowed

### Motion

- Character: expressive
- Transitions: dynamic (200-400ms), may include scale, rotation, or parallax
- Hero animations: allowed and encouraged (text reveals, product entrances)
- Scroll-triggered interactions for key conversion moments
- Loading states: energetic (not boring spinners)

### Imagery

- Mode: product screenshots with energy, abstract geometric shapes, mixed media
- Bold graphic elements: arrows, highlights, circles, lines as design elements
- Photography: action-oriented, dynamic angles
- Illustrations: bold, flat, high-contrast
- No muted, subdued, or calm imagery

## DS Token Mapping

- `data-visual`: `glass` or custom energetic variant
- `data-density`: `balanced` to `spacious`
- Shape tokens: accent borders, potential glow
- Shadow tokens: accent-tinted, higher elevation
- Motion tokens: longer, more dynamic curves

## What This Preset Avoids

- Calm, subdued, quiet aesthetics
- Corporate safety and predictability
- Minimal typography without visual impact
- Muted colors and gentle gradients
- Passive, reading-first layouts without conversion energy
