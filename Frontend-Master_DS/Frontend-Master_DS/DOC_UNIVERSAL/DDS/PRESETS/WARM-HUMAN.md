# Preset: Warm Human

Soft, approachable, friendly aesthetic. Pages feel inviting and accessible.

## Character

- warm
- approachable
- friendly

## When To Use

- Consumer-facing products
- Community platforms
- Health, wellness, or education products
- Onboarding flows that need to feel welcoming
- Products targeting non-technical audiences
- Any context where trust is built through warmth, not authority

## Visual Rules

### Typography

- Posture: balanced brand display
- Headlines: friendly weight, rounded letterforms preferred
- Body: comfortable sizes with generous line height
- Display text: warm and inviting, not aggressive
- Tone: conversational, never corporate-stiff

### Color

- Accent: warm tones — coral, amber, warm orange, soft teal, sage green
- Backgrounds: warm whites, soft creams, light peaches, gentle pastels
- Contrast: sufficient for accessibility but not harsh
- Gradients: soft, warm gradients allowed (sunset tones, warm-to-cool blends)

### Shape

- Language: soft
- Radius: larger radius (12-20px), approaching pill shapes for small elements
- Cards: rounded corners, soft shadows, warm surface tones
- Buttons: pill or rounded rectangle, inviting click feel
- Icons: rounded or filled styles, friendly illustration weight

### Surface

- Depth: layered with warmth
- Shadows: warm-tinted shadows (not pure gray)
- Borders: gentle or absent — shadows and spacing define boundaries
- Surface texture: clean but may include subtle warm overlays

### Motion

- Character: expressive but gentle
- Transitions: smooth, slightly playful (250-400ms)
- Micro-interactions encouraged (button feedback, hover warmth)
- Entry animations: soft fades and gentle slides, no aggressive transforms
- Loading states: friendly (progress indicators, skeleton screens with warm tones)

### Imagery

- Mode: editorial photography with human subjects, or friendly illustrations
- People in images are natural, not corporate-posed
- Illustrations are hand-drawn or organic style
- Avoid sterile stock photos or purely abstract imagery

## DS Token Mapping

- `data-visual`: default or warm variant
- `data-density`: `balanced` to `spacious`
- Shape tokens: increased radius values
- Shadow tokens: warm-tinted, soft
- Motion tokens: slightly longer, smoother curves

## What This Preset Avoids

- Cold corporate feel
- Sharp edges and high contrast
- Dark modes as primary (can be offered as option)
- Technical or developer-oriented visual language
- Aggressive CTA styling
