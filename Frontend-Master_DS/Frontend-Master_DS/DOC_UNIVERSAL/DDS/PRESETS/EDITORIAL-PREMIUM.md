# Preset: Editorial Premium

Spacious, typographic, magazine-inspired aesthetic. Pages feel curated and intentional.

## Character

- editorial
- premium
- refined

## When To Use

- Content-led brands and publications
- SaaS products that want to feel premium, not just functional
- About pages and company stories
- Case studies and thought leadership content
- Products with an editorial or media component

## Visual Rules

### Typography

- Posture: editorial display-heavy
- Headlines: larger than standard, may use serif or distinctive display font
- Body: generous line height, comfortable reading width (60-70ch)
- Display text: bold statements allowed — size contrast is a feature
- Pull quotes and callouts as first-class typographic elements

### Color

- Accent: muted, sophisticated tones — deep blues, warm grays, olive, burgundy
- Backgrounds: off-white, warm cream, or deep dark surfaces (not pure black)
- Contrast: deliberate — high contrast for headlines, softer for body text
- Gradients: subtle, editorial (not neon or saturated)

### Shape

- Language: soft to balanced
- Radius: gentler curves (12-16px) or intentionally sharp (0-4px) — avoid middle ground
- Cards: minimal borders, rely on spacing and typography to define blocks
- Buttons: may have distinct personality (pill, square, or custom shape)

### Surface

- Depth: layered
- Shadows: soft, diffused, creating depth without heaviness
- Borders: minimal — spacing does the work
- Surface texture: subtle paper-like or grain effects if supported by DS

### Motion

- Character: crisp
- Transitions: smooth, medium-speed (200-400ms)
- Scroll-triggered reveals for hero and key sections
- No bouncy or playful animations — elegant entrances only

### Imagery

- Mode: editorial photography or mixed media collage
- High-quality, art-directed images preferred
- Images may bleed to edges or overlap grid lines
- Mono or duotone treatments for consistency

## DS Token Mapping

- `data-visual`: default or custom editorial variant
- `data-density`: `spacious`
- Shape tokens: adjusted radius values
- Shadow tokens: softer, more diffused
- Motion tokens: slightly slower, smoother

## What This Preset Avoids

- Generic stock photography
- Bright neon accents
- Busy decorative patterns
- Dashboard-like density on public pages
- Uniform card grids without typographic variation
